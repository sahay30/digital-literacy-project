SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS

As a first-year student, it is important to use social media responsibly and maintain a positive digital presence.


DO’S:

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all your social media accounts.
2. Keep your profile professional and appropriate for academic and career purposes.
3. Think carefully before posting anything, as it stays online permanently.
4. Respect others and communicate politely in comments and messages.
5. Use privacy settings to protect your personal information.
6. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
7. Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.


DON’TS:

1. Do not share your OTP, passwords, or sensitive personal information.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
4. Do not accept friend requests from strangers.
5. Do not overshare personal details like phone number or address.
6. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
7. Do not spread rumours or unverified information.
