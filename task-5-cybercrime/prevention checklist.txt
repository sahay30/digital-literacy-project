STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
3. Always verify the sender before making any transaction.
4. Use only trusted and official apps for online payments.
5. Do not click on suspicious links or messages.
6. Enable two-factor authentication for better security.
7. Regularly check your bank account statements.
8. Immediately report any fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
